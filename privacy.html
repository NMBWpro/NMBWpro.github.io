Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Registro CS — Customer Service Case Tracker ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Google Apps Script application.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses only the following data:

  • Google Sheets data — The App reads and writes data within the Google Sheets spreadsheet that the user explicitly opens and authorizes. This data consists solely of case records entered by the user (e.g., case ID, date, description, assigned agent).

The App does not collect personal information beyond what the user enters directly into the spreadsheet.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data accessed through Google Sheets is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • To create, read, update, and organize Customer Service case records within the user's own spreadsheet.

All data processing takes place within the user's own Google account environment. No data is transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any external server or third-party service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third parties under any circumstances. Your spreadsheet data remains entirely within your Google account and is never accessed by anyone other than you.

4. Data Retention

The App does not retain or store any data outside of your Google Sheets spreadsheet. You have full control over your data at all times and may delete it directly from your spreadsheet whenever you choose.

5. Google API Services

This App uses Google API Services. Use of data obtained via Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Security

The App operates entirely within the Google Apps Script environment, which is secured by Google's own infrastructure. We do not store credentials, tokens, or any user data outside of the Google ecosystem.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: nomorebusywork.contact@gmail.com
