Registro CS — Customer Service Case Tracker

Registro CS is a Google Apps Script application designed to log and track Customer Service cases during weekends and public holidays. It helps CS teams record incidents, manage case assignments, and keep a structured log directly within Google Sheets.

What does this app do?

The script runs inside a Google Sheets spreadsheet. When authorized, it reads and writes data to the spreadsheet to create, update, and organize CS case records. No data is sent to external servers — all information stays within the user's own Google account.

Data accessed

  • Google Sheets — to read and write case records in the spreadsheet.

Who can use this app?

This application is intended for Customer Service teams and individuals who purchase or are granted access to the spreadsheet template.
